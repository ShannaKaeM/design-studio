AFTER PASTING, SWITCH TO VISUAL EDITOR AND:

1. Click into the container you just added
2. In the block settings sidebar:
   
   SPACING TAB:
   - Padding: Set all to 0
   
3. Click the + inside the container to add another Container block inside it

4. For the INNER container:
   
   ADVANCED TAB:
   - Additional CSS class(es): gb-container hero-section
   
   BACKGROUNDS TAB:
   - Add your background image
   
   SPACING TAB:
   - Margin Top: 10 (set unit to %)
   - Margin Bottom: 10 (set unit to %)
   - Padding: 60px all around
   
   LAYOUT TAB:
   - Min Height: 350 (px)
   - Display: flex
   - Direction: column
   - Align Items: center
   - Justify Content: center
   
5. Inside this container, add:
   - A Headline block (set to H1, add class: hero-title, color: white, center align)
   - A Paragraph block (add class: hero-description, color: white, center align)
